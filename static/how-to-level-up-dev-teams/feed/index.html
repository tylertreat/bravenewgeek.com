By: foo https://bravenewgeek.com/how-to-level-up-dev-teams/#comment-24612 Sun, 06 Jan 2019 13:26:36 +0000 https://bravenewgeek.com/how-to-level-up-dev-teams/#comment-24612 If the only thing you do to level-up is code reviews (which are great), then soon you won’t learn much beyond what is known by reviewers inside the company.

Considering that, in general, almost all knowledge on a tech topic is actually outside the company, only looking inside for knowledge quickly becomes a serious limitation. Worse, it leads to monoculture and NIH!

Plus, different people (or the same people, but at different skill or motivation levels) learn more effectively in different ways.

Of course, wasting money on useless training sessions happens more often than not, and books can’t give practical experience; it doesn’t mean that these are not useful, important learning tools.

The more tools (in the broadest sense) you have, and the more you look outside for knowledge and for help, the better.
